### What is Stainless Steel Screen Partition?
Stainless steel screen partitions are decorative and functional panels made from high-grade stainless steel (such as 201, 304, or 316).
They are widely used in hotels, villas, commercial spaces, and luxury interiors to create spatial separation while maintaining visual openness.
These screens can be customized with laser cutting, PVD coating, electroplating, and antique finishes, offering both durability and premium aesthetics.
### Specifications
- Material: Stainless Steel (201 / 304 / 316)
- Thickness: 0.5mm – 3mm
- Surface Finish: Mirror / Brushed / Hairline / PVD Coating
- Color: Gold / Rose Gold / Black / Bronze / Custom
- Processing: Laser Cutting / Welding / Polishing
- Size: Customizable
- Installation: Fixed / Embedded / Modular

### FAQ
Q1: What is the best material for decorative screens?
A: 304 stainless steel is the most commonly used due to its balance of corrosion resistance and cost.
Q2: Can stainless steel screens be used outdoors?
A: Yes, especially 316 stainless steel which has excellent resistance to harsh environments.
Q3: What thickness is recommended?
A: Typically between 0.8mm and 2.0mm depending on design and structure.
Q4: Are stainless steel screens customizable?
A: Yes, patterns, sizes, and finishes can all be customized.
Q5: How to maintain stainless steel screens?
A: Simply clean with a soft cloth and mild detergent.
