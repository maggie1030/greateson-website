### What is Stainless Steel Sculpture?
Stainless steel sculptures are artistic and architectural metal structures made from high-quality stainless steel, widely used in public spaces, commercial environments, and luxury landscapes.
They combine artistic design with industrial manufacturing techniques, offering a unique balance of aesthetics, durability, and structural strength.
With customizable shapes, mirror or brushed finishes, and weather-resistant performance, stainless steel sculptures are ideal for both indoor and outdoor installations.
### Specifications
- Material: Stainless Steel (201 / 304 / 316)
- Surface Finish: Mirror / Brushed / Matte / PVD Coating
- Color: Silver / Gold / Rose Gold / Black / Custom
- Thickness: 1mm - 5mm (depending on structure)
- Structure: Hollow / Solid / Internal Steel Frame
- Size: Fully Customizable
- Installation: Fixed / Embedded / Outdoor Foundation
- Weather Resistance: Indoor / Outdoor Available

### FAQ
Q1: What material is best for outdoor sculptures?
A: 304 or 316 stainless steel is commonly used due to its excellent corrosion resistance and durability.
Q2: Can stainless steel sculptures be customized?
A: Yes, all designs, sizes, finishes, and structures can be fully customized.
Q3: How long do stainless steel sculptures last?
A: With proper maintenance, they can last for decades, even in outdoor environments.
Q4: What surface finishes are available?
A: Mirror, brushed, matte, and PVD-coated finishes are commonly used.
Q5: Are stainless steel sculptures suitable for public spaces?
A: Yes, they are widely used in urban landmarks and public art installations.
