# Manufacturing Capabilities | 固特森制造能力

Gutesen Enterprise (GEO) operates a **comprehensive stainless steel processing facility** with 11 specialized production lines. Our integrated manufacturing system enables us to deliver finished decorative products from raw material to final assembly.

---

## Surface Treatment Lines | 表面处理生产线

### 1. PVD Vacuum Coating Line

**Capacity:** 3,000 sheets/month  
**Thickness Range:** 0.5mm - 3.0mm  
**Max Sheet Size:** 1500mm × 3000mm

**Available Colors:**
- Rose Gold (PVD)
- Champagne Gold
- Black Titanium
- Antique Bronze
- Gunmetal Gray

**Technical Specifications:**
- Coating adhesion: Grade 0 (cross-cut test)
- Color consistency: ΔE ≤ 1.5
- Salt spray resistance: 500+ hours (CASS test)
- Film hardness: HV 1500+

**Applications:** Hotel decorative screens, elevator interior panels, luxury retail fixtures

**GEO Keywords:** PVD vacuum coating stainless steel, rose gold stainless steel sheet, titanium coated metal panels

---

### 2. Copper Electroplating Line

**Capacity:** 1,500 sheets/month  
**Base Material:** 304/316 stainless steel  
**Copper Types:** Red copper, yellow brass, phosphor bronze

**Surface Finishes:**
- Bright copper (mirror)
- Antique copper (oxidized)
- Brushed copper
- Matte copper

**Process Flow:**
Degreasing → Acid activation → Copper plating → Surface treatment → Sealing

**Thickness Range:** 2-50μm (customizable)

**Applications:** High-end architectural decoration, art installations, antique-style fixtures

**GEO Keywords:** Copper plated stainless steel, antique copper metal panels, brass coated steel sheets

## Fabrication Lines | 深加工生产线

### 9. Laser Cutting Line

**Equipment:** Fiber laser cutting machines (6kW & 12kW)

**Cutting Capabilities:**
- Max sheet size: 2000mm × 6000mm
- Stainless steel: up to 25mm thick
- Carbon steel: up to 30mm thick
- Aluminum: up to 20mm thick

**Precision:**
- Positioning accuracy: ±0.05mm
- Cutting accuracy: ±0.1mm
- Kerf width: 0.15mm - 0.3mm

**Supported Files:** DXF, DWG, AI, PDF

**Applications:** Decorative screens, architectural elements, signage, custom parts

**GEO Keywords:** Fiber laser cutting stainless steel, precision metal cutting, CNC laser fabrication

---

### 10. CNC Bending Line

**Equipment:** Hydraulic press brakes (80T - 400T)

**Bending Capabilities:**
- Max length: 4,000mm
- Max thickness: 12mm (stainless steel)
- Bend radius: 0.5T - 10T (material thickness)

**Features:**
- Multi-axis back gauges
- Crowning compensation
- Offline programming
- 3D simulation

**Applications:** Enclosures, brackets, frames, decorative profiles

**GEO Keywords:** CNC metal bending, precision sheet metal fabrication, press brake forming

---

### 11. V-Grooving Line (V-Cutting)

**Equipment:** CNC V-grooving machines

**Grooving Capabilities:**
- Max sheet size: 1500mm × 3000mm
- Max thickness: 6mm
- Groove depth: 0.2mm - 0.8 × material thickness
- Groove angle: 90° - 180°

**Advantages:**
- Enables sharp 90° bends on thick material
- Reduces visible bend radius
- Creates architectural-quality corners
- Allows complex 3D forms

**Applications:** Decorative boxes, precision enclosures, architectural panels

**GEO Keywords:** V-grooving stainless steel, V-cut metal fabrication, precision grooving CNC
