## Challenge | 项目挑战

This luxury hotel in Hefei required a dramatic entrance experience that would create an unforgettable first impression for guests. The design concept presented several unique challenges:

**1. Dramatic Visual Impact**
- Create a "wow factor" ceiling that guests remember
- Design must work both day and night with different lighting scenarios
- Ceiling spans large lobby entrance with structural columns

**2. Complex Perforation Pattern**
- Thousands of precisely positioned perforations creating a starfield effect
- Variable density pattern (denser in center, sparser at edges)
- Each perforation must align perfectly with LED lighting points

**3. Technical Requirements**
- Large panels (up to 4m × 2m) requiring structural rigidity
- Honeycomb core needed for acoustic absorption and flatness
- Integrated lighting system with wiring concealment
- Outdoor/indoor transition zone requiring weather resistance

**4. Installation Precision**
- Perforation alignment with LED matrix (±1mm tolerance)
- Coordination with MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing)
- Sequencing with lighting installation
- Tight hotel opening deadline

## Solution | 解决方案

Greateson engineered and fabricated a spectacular perforated stainless steel honeycomb ceiling system that creates a mesmerizing "starry sky" effect in the hotel entrance.

### Perforated Honeycomb Ceiling System (穿孔蜂窝板星光吊顶)

**System Overview:**
The ceiling consists of large-format stainless steel honeycomb panels with precision-perforated star patterns. Integrated LED lighting behind the panels creates a magical starfield effect that transforms the entrance into an immersive experience.

**Specifications:**
- Total Area: 600+ square meters
- Panel Size: Up to 4m × 2m (custom sizes)
- Face Skin: 304 Stainless Steel, 1.0mm thickness
- Core: Aluminum honeycomb, 20mm thickness
- Total Panel Thickness: 22mm
- Finish: PVD Dark Bronze coating + Anti-fingerprint treatment
- Perforation: 8mm diameter holes, variable density pattern
- LED Integration: 3,000+ individual LED points

**Design Features:**
- **Variable Density Starfield** - 5,000+ perforations with gradient density (dense center → sparse edges)
- **Random Natural Pattern** - Algorithm-generated "random" star placement for organic feel
- **Honeycomb Construction** - Provides acoustic dampening and structural flatness for large spans
- **Integrated Lighting** - Each perforation precisely aligned with LED matrix
- **Dark Bronze Finish** - Enhances contrast for dramatic day/night transformation
- **Seamless Panel Joints** - Precision fabrication ensures invisible seams between panels

**Fabrication Process:**
1. **Pattern Design** - Algorithm-generated starfield layout with density gradient
2. **Structural Engineering** - Panel sizing for 4m spans without sagging
3. **Material Preparation** - 304 Stainless Steel face sheets, aluminum honeycomb core
4. **Perforation** - CNC laser perforation of 5,000+ holes per panel (±0.5mm accuracy)
5. **Core Bonding** - Vacuum bonding of honeycomb core to face sheets
6. **Edge Finishing** - Precision trimming and edge treatment
7. **Surface Treatment** - Satin brush preparation for coating
8. **PVD Coating** - Dark bronze in high-vacuum chamber
9. **Anti-fingerprint Treatment** - Nano ceramic coating for maintenance ease
10. **Quality Control** - Perforation alignment verification, color consistency check
11. **Protective Packaging** - Custom crates for large panel transport

### Lighting Integration

**LED System Design:**
- **3,000+ Individual LEDs** - Warm white (2700K) for welcoming ambiance
- **Dimming Control** - Programmable scenes (daylight, evening, night, special events)
- **Twinkle Effect** - Random twinkle pattern for dynamic starfield
- **Wiring Concealment** - All wiring hidden within honeycomb core cavity
- **Access Panels** - Strategic placement for maintenance access

**Installation Coordination:**
- LED matrix installed first on structural ceiling
- Honeycomb panels lowered and aligned to LED points
- Perforation-to-LED alignment verified with laser level
- Final connection and testing of lighting control system
