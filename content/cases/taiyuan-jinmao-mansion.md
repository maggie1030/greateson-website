## Challenge | 项目挑战

The Taiyuan Jinmao Mansion is a prestigious luxury residential development, and its sales center needed to convey exclusivity and sophistication from the moment visitors arrive. The design requirements presented several challenges:

**1. Grand Entrance Impact**
- Create an impressive first impression for potential homebuyers
- Feature wall must embody luxury and craftsmanship
- Design should complement the water feature and stone columns

**2. Architectural Integration**
- Grille panels needed to span from floor to ceiling (approximately 6 meters)
- Must integrate seamlessly with existing marble columns and water landscape
- Required precise alignment with the building's contemporary aesthetic

**3. Technical Specifications**
- Large-scale vertical grille with consistent spacing and alignment
- Outdoor/indoor transition zone exposure (weather resistance required)
- High-traffic commercial environment requiring durable finish
- Reflective water feature demanded superior surface quality

**4. Timeline Pressure**
- Sales center opening deadline tied to property pre-sales launch
- Installation coordination with multiple trades (water feature, lighting, stone work)

---

## Solution | 解决方案

Greateson designed and fabricated a stunning floor-to-ceiling stainless steel grille system that serves as the signature architectural element of the sales center entrance.

### Custom Vertical Grille System (不锈钢隔栅)

**Specifications:**
- Total Height: 6.2 meters
- Total Length: 15 meters (single integrated unit)
- Material: 304 Stainless Steel
- Profile: Custom extruded vertical bars with integrated horizontal connectors
- Finish: PVD Champagne Gold coating + Anti-fingerprint treatment (integrated coloring)
- Bar Dimensions: 80mm width × 15mm thickness
- Spacing: 40mm gaps between bars
- Construction: Fully welded frame, integrated PVD coating after assembly

**Design Features:**
- **Monolithic Structure** - Single-unit fabrication spanning 15 meters without visible seams
- **Rhythm Pattern** - Vertical bars create elegant visual rhythm throughout full length
- **Intermittent Horizontal Bars** - Strategic horizontal connectors add geometric interest and structural stability
- **Floating Effect** - Supported by concealed floor and ceiling fixings
- **Lighting Integration** - Channels for concealed LED strip lighting (installed by others)
- **Unified Color** - Integrated PVD coating ensures perfect color consistency from end to end

**Fabrication Process:**
1. **3D Site Survey** - Precise measurements of the curved lobby entrance
2. **Structural Design** - Engineering calculations for 15-meter span stability as single unit
3. **Material Preparation** - 304 Stainless Steel flat bars, 3.0mm base thickness
4. **Precision Cutting** - Fiber laser cutting for perfect bar lengths
5. **Welding Assembly** - Complete TIG welding of all vertical bars and horizontal connectors as one integrated structure
6. **Surface Grinding** - Progressive polishing to #800 mirror finish on entire assembly
7. **Integrated PVD Coating** - Entire grille placed in high-vacuum chamber for unified champagne gold coloring (ensuring perfect color consistency)
8. **Anti-fingerprint Treatment** - Nano ceramic coating applied to entire unit for maintenance ease
9. **Quality Control** - Color consistency check (ΔE < 1.0), structural integrity test
10. **Protective Packaging** - Custom wooden crate for transport as single unit

### Installation Coordination

**Mounting System:**
- Concealed floor channels (stainless steel U-channels embedded in stone) supporting full 15-meter span
- Top restraint brackets connected to ceiling structure at multiple points
- No visible seams or joints - continuous visual flow from end to end

**Coordination Requirements:**
- Alignment with water feature nozzles (precision to ±2mm)
- Integration with column cladding (same material and finish)
- Sequencing with final stone and lighting installation
