## Challenge | 项目挑战

This premium residential development in Beijing required a sales center that would stand out in the competitive luxury property market. The architectural vision called for a distinctive exterior that would:

**1. Iconic First Impression**
- Create a memorable architectural landmark visible from the street
- Reflect the premium positioning of the residential development
- Function as a "billboard" for the property's quality and design standards

**2. Complex Geometry**
- Curved building facade requiring custom panel fabrication
- Integration with LED lighting system for dramatic night-time presence
- Multiple levels and angled surfaces to cover

**3. Weather Resistance**
- Beijing's extreme climate: hot summers, cold winters, heavy pollution
- Exterior exposure requiring superior corrosion resistance
- UV stability for color retention over years

**4. Installation Complexity**
- Large-scale facade installation on curved surfaces
- Coordination with lighting contractors
- Tight construction schedule to meet sales launch deadline

## Solution | 解决方案

Greateson designed and fabricated a stunning stainless steel "Great Wall" panel facade system that creates a distinctive architectural identity for the sales center.

### Great Wall Panel System (不锈钢长城板)

**What is Great Wall Panel?**
Great Wall panels (长城板) feature a distinctive vertical rib pattern with alternating convex and concave sections, creating a rhythmic shadow effect reminiscent of the Great Wall of China. This profile adds depth, texture, and visual interest to building facades.

**Specifications:**
- Total Area: 800+ square meters
- Panel Height: Custom lengths up to 6 meters
- Material: 304 Stainless Steel
- Profile: Great Wall pattern with 50mm pitch (25mm convex + 25mm concave)
- Thickness: 1.5mm base material
- Finish: PVD Champagne Gold coating + Anti-fingerprint treatment
- Rib Depth: 15mm

**Design Features:**
- **Rhythmic Pattern** - Alternating convex/concave ribs create dynamic shadow play
- **Curved Adaptation** - Panels formed to follow building's organic curves
- **Integrated Lighting** - Top and bottom LED channels for dramatic illumination
- **Seamless Corners** - Custom-formed corner pieces maintain pattern continuity
- **Unified Finish** - Integrated PVD coating ensures perfect color consistency

**Fabrication Process:**
1. **Architectural Coordination** - 3D modeling of curved facade geometry
2. **Panel Design** - Custom tooling for Great Wall profile on curved surfaces
3. **Material Preparation** - 304 Stainless Steel coils, 1.5mm thickness
4. **Roll Forming** - Precision forming of Great Wall profile
5. **Curved Forming** - CNC press brake for radius adaptation
6. **Surface Treatment** - Progressive polishing to #400 satin finish
7. **PVD Coating** - Champagne gold in high-vacuum chamber
8. **Anti-fingerprint Treatment** - Nano ceramic coating for exterior durability
9. **Quality Control** - Color consistency check (ΔE < 1.5), profile accuracy verification
10. **Protective Packaging** - Custom wooden crates for transport

### Installation System

**Mounting Structure:**
- Aluminum support frame with adjustable brackets
- Concealed fastening system (no visible screws)
- Expansion joints to accommodate thermal movement
- Waterproof membrane behind panels

**Lighting Integration:**
- LED strip channels at panel top edges
- LED strip channels at panel bottom edges
- Uplighting and downlighting for dramatic effect
- Warm color temperature (3000K) to complement champagne gold
