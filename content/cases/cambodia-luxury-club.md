## Challenge | 项目挑战

The client envisioned an ultra-luxury private club that would set a new standard for premium entertainment venues in Phnom Penh. The design concept required:

**1. Unique Aesthetic Requirements**
- Curved petal-shaped tables as signature design elements
- Seamless column cladding with flowing organic forms
- Consistent luxury finish throughout the venue
- Integration with dramatic lighting systems

**2. Technical Challenges**
- Complex 3D curved geometries requiring precision fabrication
- Consistent surface finish across all pieces (PVD coating durability)
- Tight installation schedule to meet venue opening
- Coordination with curved LED lighting integration

**3. Quality Expectations**
- Mirror-grade surface reflection quality
- Perfect seam alignment for curved joints
- Scratch-resistant finish for high-traffic commercial use
- Color consistency across 20+ large components

---

## Solution | 解决方案

Greateson provided comprehensive custom fabrication services, delivering a complete stainless steel decorative solution:

### Custom Petal Tables (花瓣台)

**Specifications:**
- Quantity: 12 units
- Material: 304 Stainless Steel, 1.5mm thickness
- Finish: PVD Champagne Gold coating + Anti-fingerprint treatment
- Diameter: 800mm - 1200mm (various sizes)
- Features: Integrated LED lighting channels

**Fabrication Process:**
1. **3D Modeling** - Precise CAD modeling of petal curves
2. **Laser Cutting** - High-precision fiber laser for petal shapes
3. **CNC Bending** - Multi-axis forming for 3D curves
4. **Welding & Grinding** - Seamless joints, mirror polish
5. **PVD Coating** - Champagne gold in vacuum chamber
6. **Anti-fingerprint Treatment** - Nano coating for easy maintenance

### Column Cladding (柱子包覆)

**Specifications:**
- Quantity: 8 large columns
- Material: 304 Stainless Steel, 2.0mm thickness
- Finish: PVD Champagne Gold + Hairline brush combination
- Height: 4.2 meters each
- Design: Flowing organic curves with integrated lighting

**Technical Highlights:**
- **V-Grooving Technology** - Enabled sharp 90° bends for seamless edges
- **Segmented Fabrication** - Columns assembled on-site in 3 sections
- **Lighting Integration** - Pre-cut channels for LED strip installation
- **Structural Support** - Internal aluminum framework for rigidity

### Decorative Ceiling Elements

**Specifications:**
- Area: 200+ square meters of ceiling cladding
- Material: Stainless steel with PVD coating
- Finish: Champagne gold, satin brushed
- Features: Curved forms matching column design language
