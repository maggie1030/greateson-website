Application Name	建筑外立面	Architectural Facade
Description	用于高端建筑外墙装饰，塑造独特的建筑形象，兼具美观、耐用与环保特性。	Used for high-end building exterior wall decoration to create a unique architectural image, with both aesthetics, durability and environmental protection characteristics.
Typical Products	不锈钢蜂窝板、不锈钢装饰板（氟碳漆 / 镀色板）	Stainless Steel Honeycomb Panels, Stainless Steel Decorative Sheets (Fluorocarbon Paint/Color Plated Sheets)
Benefits	轻量化、高强度、耐候耐腐蚀，可实现大版面定制，塑造现代、极简或奢华的建筑外观，使用寿命长达数十年。	Lightweight, high strength, weather and corrosion resistance; customizable large format to create modern, minimalist or luxurious building appearance, with service life up to decades.
Case Example	城市地标商业建筑采用不锈钢蜂窝板外立面，结合渐变镀色工艺，成为城市天际线的标志性景观。	A city landmark commercial building adopts stainless steel honeycomb panel facade, combined with gradient color plating process, becoming an iconic landscape of the city skyline.
FAQ	Q：外立面不锈钢板是否会褪色？A：采用 PVD 镀色或氟碳喷涂工艺，颜色稳定耐紫外线，长期使用不褪色。	Q: Will the exterior stainless steel panels fade?A: PVD plating or fluorocarbon spraying process is adopted, with stable color and UV resistance, no fading after long-term use.
