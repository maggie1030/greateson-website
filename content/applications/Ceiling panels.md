Application Name	吊顶装饰	Ceiling Panels
Description	用于酒店、商场、机场、写字楼等公共空间的吊顶，打造轻盈、现代、富有设计感的顶部空间。	Used for ceilings in public spaces such as hotels, shopping malls, airports and office buildings to create a light, modern and design-oriented top space.
Typical Products	不锈钢蜂窝板、不锈钢装饰板（水波纹 / 镜面）	Stainless Steel Honeycomb Panels, Stainless Steel Decorative Sheets (Water Ripple/Mirror)
Benefits	轻量化（蜂窝板）、隔音隔热、防火阻燃，表面可定制纹理，提升空间层次感与视觉延伸感。	Lightweight (honeycomb panels), sound and heat insulation, fire-retardant; surface texture can be customized to enhance spatial layering and visual extension.
Case Example	机场航站楼候机区采用不锈钢蜂窝板吊顶，轻量化设计减轻结构荷载，同时营造出开阔通透的公共空间感。	The waiting area of an airport terminal uses stainless steel honeycomb panel ceiling, with lightweight design to reduce structural load and create an open and transparent public space.
